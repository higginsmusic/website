Multimark Media

Multimark Media is the core component of all of my media projects moving forward.

As I look to expand into other formats of media beyond audio recording, and as I refine my video production skills and begin to develop web applications, I felt it was appropriate to reframe my approach to the studio and how I view the integration of it all.

It is my goal to provide full media production and web packages for artists in the short term.

Higgins Music

Higgins Music is the current evolution of my personal works. I am certain it will not be the final iteration.

I have a life full of musical stories and associations. I may not be the most well known musician you will meet, but I am rather accomplished. I began recording my music at age 12, after learning how to operate a mixer for my father's singing gigs and realizing I could hook up a tape deck and multi-track through the mixer. I still have my very first synth and still love the way it sounds. Of course, I have come a long way since my early days and have gained experience playing on all kinds of stages from punk to electronica to jazz bar to classical to rock and roll. I have also been a major contributor to many recording projects along the years with the different groups I have performed in. This diversity of experience and influence is apparant in my original work.

Higgins Music is intentionally the insight to the "right now" moments. Some daydreams and fantasy storylines with the reality of life poking you right in the eye. Maybe some lingering hurt searching for relief. Maybe some reminisce to carefree childhood days.
